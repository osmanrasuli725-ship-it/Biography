About Me

I am Usman Rasuli, an independent and responsible individual. I face life with patience and determination, never thinking of giving up when challenges arise. I constantly work on improving myself through reading, exercising, and learning new skills. My greatest strength is being able to create my own path without depending on others, while always striving to become a better version of myself.

Early Life & Background

I come from an Afghan family that describes me as intelligent and mature. My childhood was filled with moments of togetherness—traveling, spending time with my family, and supporting one another. Throughout my education, I have been a curious and motivated student, especially interested in business, English, and history. I’ve always enjoyed sharing ideas, leading discussions, and expressing my thoughts clearly, which helped me grow both academically and personally.

My Dreams & Goals

In the future, I aim to become a successful university student and develop a strong entrepreneurial vision. My goal is to create my own path through innovation and meaningful ideas while making a difference both personally and socially. My biggest dream is to build a world where people help one another and celebrate success together without jealousy or competition.
